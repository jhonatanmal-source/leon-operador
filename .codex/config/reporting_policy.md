# Reporting Policy

## Required Content
Every report must include:
1. Executive Summary
2. Status (APROVADO/APROVADO COM RESSALVAS/REPROVADO/BLOQUEADO)
3. Environment
4. Actions taken
5. Files created/modified
6. Tests executed and results
7. Errors found
8. Risks identified
9. Pending items
10. Next steps

## Rules
- Base every statement on real evidence
- Do not invent results
- Mark uncertainties clearly
- Differentiate facts from hypotheses
