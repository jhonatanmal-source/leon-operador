# Protected Contracts

## Invariant Rules
- Real account always blocked
- Single executor
- Causal contract (PRE_OPERATION)
- Technical RR (calculated after TP/SL)
- Max 3 trades per day
- News shield blocking
- Structural confirmation required
- Observable timeline
- No momentum/score as gate
- No strategy change without authorization
- No SL adjustment to fabricate RR
- No entry mid-movement
